# Skill: /growth

## Trigger
`/growth [positioning, landing copy, pricing, acquisition, SEO, launch plan]`

## Purpose
Help a solo founder turn product work into clear positioning, acquisition
experiments, and launch messaging.

## Use when

- defining ICP and positioning
- writing landing page copy
- planning SEO content
- designing onboarding emails
- testing pricing or packaging ideas
- preparing launch posts or outreach

## Process

1. Identify the target customer and job-to-be-done.
2. State the product promise in plain language.
3. Separate proof, assumptions, and claims.
4. Propose the smallest marketing experiment.
5. Keep copy truthful to the current product.

## Output

```
## Growth Plan: [goal]

### ICP
- customer

### Positioning
- one-sentence promise

### Message
- headline
- supporting copy

### Experiment
- channel
- action
- success metric

### Risks
- assumption to validate
```

## Guardrails

- never invent traction, revenue, customers, benchmarks, or testimonials
- do not overpromise features that are not built
- prefer one measurable experiment over a broad marketing plan
- keep claims grounded in current product reality
